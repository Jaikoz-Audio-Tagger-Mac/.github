## What is Jaikoz Audio Tagger Mac?

Jaikoz Audio Tagger Mac is a **professional metadata editing tool** specifically designed for macOS users. It enables musicians, collectors, and audio enthusiasts to manage, edit, and organize audio file tags with ease. Supporting formats like MP3, FLAC, OGG, and M4A, Jaikoz simplifies the process of cleaning up and standardizing music libraries.

The software includes **batch processing capabilities**, allowing users to tag hundreds of files simultaneously. With integrated acoustic fingerprinting, Jaikoz automatically identifies tracks and retrieves accurate metadata from online databases such as MusicBrainz and Discogs. This ensures that your audio files are always correctly labeled with artist, album, year, genre, and cover art.

Jaikoz is fully compatible with **macOS Sonoma, Ventura, Monterey, and earlier versions**, and runs efficiently on both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs. Its intuitive interface makes it accessible for beginners, while powerful features like regex support and custom scripts cater to advanced users.

Whether you're preparing a DJ set, archiving a vinyl collection, or simply organizing your personal music library, Jaikoz delivers precision and reliability.
